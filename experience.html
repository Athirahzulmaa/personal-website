Participate in the Drawing Competition to Represent the School

I really like art, no matter it's painting, graphic design, illustration, sculpture, literature, architecture, film, music, theater, and fashion. But what I am most interested in is fashion design and fine art. Once during form 5, we attended an art company to hold a young talent competition and a school art workshop. At that point, no one wanted to represent the school to the competition, probably because they were not confident. So I offered myself to participate in the competition. Despite only getting a certificate of participation, I was quite happy to be able to represent the school that was then hosting the art workshop and get a feel for how to follow the school level competition. There, I gained the experience of drawing something I had never drawn before, being able to follow the competition and see how each student’s process of drawing. It's really fun.

Be a hawker

My parents were a trader in Jed Street, Bandar Jengka, who sold apam back. As the eldest child, I am sure I need to help them trade. And I had the opportunity to feel like a hawker and at the same time an entrepreneur. It was quite fun to learn how to sell, cook and communicate with customers that of course I didn’t know.

University entrance experience

I was born into a family that didn't know much about technology and universities. At first I wanted to apply for UPU, I really tried to find a way to apply whatever I felt suited my result, at that time I really didn’t know about websites and blogs that could help me to get a lot of information. I am grateful to know that I graduated from UiTM under the IM110 program. I was also very excited when stepping into the Machang campus at that time. I learned many new things while at university. Despite many challenges, I am grateful for my independent ability to survive for 6 semesters in Kelantan.
